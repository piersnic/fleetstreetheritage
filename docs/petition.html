From 14-17 September 2023, we had an event as part of en House London. We exhibited 56 of our Heritage panels printed on plastic and mounted on a light wooden framework. These gave an impression of how ceramic panels would look on a wall like this.

We asked all our visitors to sign the petition given below, and168 of them did so. The petition read “We urge the City of London Corporation to do everything in its power to establish a permanent,, openly accessible exhibition of ceramic tiles incorporating as many as possible of the Fleet Street Heritage information panels as displayed at the Open House 2023”

We have now started an online petition to allow all our readers to join in PLEASE SIGN OUR ONLINE PETITION
